About Us

TheCleanOrg aims at reducing waste and is taking a step towards waste management by collecting waste from our community, seggereating them into biodegradable and non biodegradable waste, and finally creating sustainable and eco-friendly products out of them.

TheCleanOrg is an endeavour to create one stop shop for meeting the needs of agriculture created from biodegradable waste to an Indian farmer. With this portal Indian Farmer will benefit by obtaining products which are eco-friendly and waste minimising in nature. Manure can be an economic “Win”, due to its fertility value, and a soil quality “Win”, due to its organic matter.

Our plan is converting the biodegradable waste that we collect into manure, to supply to the farmers.

Recycling of non-degradable material is a crucial subject considering the long-term environmental effect of waste disposal. Land filling and blazing of this waste is not the right answer for this problem. Here at TheCleanOrg, we create active wear out of the non biodegradable waste that we collect from our community.
