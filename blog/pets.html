PETs

Recycled plastic clothing is generally made from one of two plastics: polyester or nylon. These materials can be recycled from pre or post-consumer waste. Pre-consumer waste refers to factory waste that is recycled on an industrial scale; whereas post-consumer waste refers to consumer waste such as drinks bottles.

Polyester is the most widely used plastic in the world, also known by the chemical name polyethylene terephthalate (PET). It's probably best known as the material that single-use drinks bottles are made from, and this gives us a good indication about its relatively low toxicity

In its virgin form, PET is widely considered safe to wear as it is free from known endocrine disruptors such as BPA and BPS.
